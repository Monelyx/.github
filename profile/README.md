## About Monelyx

Monelyx is a workspace-native finance platform built to manage money with more context, more collaboration, and more operational depth. It combines everyday financial workflows with business-grade accounting capabilities, AI-powered analysis, and integrations with the systems that move money in the real world.

## Platform Highlights

<table>
  <tr>
    <td width="33%" valign="top">
      <strong>Workspace-First Architecture</strong><br />
      Multi-tenant by design, with access control centered on workspace membership, roles, and scoped permissions.
    </td>
    <td width="33%" valign="top">
      <strong>API-Driven Platform</strong><br />
      Structured HTTP APIs, OpenAPI documentation, generated contracts, and predictable domain boundaries.
    </td>
    <td width="33%" valign="top">
      <strong>Async and Event-Driven</strong><br />
      Scheduled jobs, webhook processing, sync pipelines, alerts, reporting, and operational automation.
    </td>
  </tr>
</table>

## Design Principles

- Build finance software that feels native to real-life collaboration, not just individual tracking
- Support both everyday financial visibility and accounting-grade operational depth
- Treat AI as an assistive layer that improves understanding and decision-making
- Favor safe retries, auditability, and idempotent workflows across integrations and background jobs
